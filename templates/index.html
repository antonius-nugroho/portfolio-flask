Introduction

About Me

Engineer with over 17 years of experience in the Indonesian power sector, combining a background in Electrical Engineering (Control Systems) with a Magister in Management.

Currently serving as Senior Officer — Operational Performance at PT PLN Nusantara Power, UP Tarahan. My career has evolved from hands-on boiler and transformer maintenance to financial and operational analysis at a strategic level.

I am actively developing expertise in Machine Learning and Python, with the goal of applying advanced analytics to operational challenges and contributing to academic research that bridges industrial feasibility with sustainable energy policy.

"What sets me apart — even past 40 — I keep learning new things: biomass technology, ML, AI. These are not just interests; they are tools I am actively preparing to deploy."
